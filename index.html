Understanding Market Structure

A simple explanation of how price moves and why it matters.

Market structure is the foundation of trading. If you understand market structure then you have solved up to 50% of the trading puzzle. Market structure is the basically the movement of price. Price moves in three major ways: Up, down and sideways. The upward movement of price is called the UPTREND. When the market is in an Uptrend, price is making a series of higher highs and higher lows. In this trend, our major focus is mainly to buy. The downward movement of price is called the DOWNTREND. When the market is in a downtrend, it means proce is making a series of lower highs and lower lows. In this trend, our major focus is to sell. The side ways movement of price is CONSOLIDATION. Here price is just moving up and down without breaking a major level. When price is consolidating, its a signal to pause and just observe. A major take away here is that, ideally we want to trade with the trend. Buy when the market is buying, sell when the market is selling and stay away when the market is consolidating.
